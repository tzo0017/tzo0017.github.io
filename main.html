Welcome to my website: I am a PhD candidate at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Auburn University. I have joined Real-time Networking lab in Fall 2018. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Yin Sun. I completed my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Auburn University in 2022. I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2017. My research includes information freshness, remote estimation, robotic control, Age of Information (AoI), wireless communication networks, and machine learning. I have worked on designing novel sampling policies for remote estimation of stochastic processes involving single and multiple source systems. In addition, my work includes design of a Whittle index policy for multi-source, multi-channel remote estimation system while each source monitors a real-time Gauss-Markov process. Currently, I am working on the analysis of an optimal scheduler for general situational-aware loss functions and to provide information-theoretic interpretation for general loss functions. I have received IEEE/IFIP WiOpt Best paper Award in 2019. I am a student member of IEEE and ACM.
