Welcome to my website: I am a Post-Doctoral Scholar at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and NSF AI-Edge Institue of Ohio State University. I have joined Ohio State University in Summer 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Ness B. Shroff. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering at Auburn University in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Yin Sun, and my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering there in 2022. I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2017. My research includes reinforcement learning, information freshness, remote estimation, robotic control, Age of Information (AoI), wireless communication networks, and machine learning. I have worked on designing novel sampling policies for remote estimation of stochastic processes involving single and multiple source systems. In addition, my work includes design of a Whittle index policy for multi-source, multi-channel remote estimation system while each source monitors a real-time Gauss-Markov process. Currently, I am working on the analysis of an optimal scheduler for general situational-aware loss functions and to provide information-theoretic interpretation for general loss functions. I have received IEEE/IFIP WiOpt Best paper Award in 2019. I am a student member of IEEE and ACM. The December 2023 issue of the ACM SIGMETRICS Performance Evaluation Review (PER) newsletter will profile an overview of my thesis.
